Primary Eduacation

I completed my primary education at Central English School in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. I studied there from class 1 to 5 and learned the basics of various subjects such as English, Hindi, Maths, Science, Social Studies and Art. I also participated in sports, cultural events and competitions. I made many friends and enjoyed my time at the school.

Secondary Education

I continued my secondary education at Kendriya Vidyalaya EME Baroda. It is a prestigious school that follows the CBSE curriculum and caters to the children of defence personnel. I studied there from class 6 to 9 half. I also took part in NCC and other co-curricular activities. I met many students from different backgrounds and cultures.

Higher Secondary Education

I completed my secondary education at Kendriya Vidyalaya Shahibaug Ahmedabad. It is another branch of the same school chain that I attended earlier. I studied there from class 9 half to 10 and prepared for the board exams. I had a memorable experience at Kendriya Vidyalaya Shahibaug Ahmedabad.

!!!Jee Time!!!

After completing my secondary education, I decided to pursue engineering as my career. I prepared for the JEE Main and Advanced exams at home with the help of Aakash Institute. I enrolled in their two-year integrated course and followed their study material and test series. I learned a lot from their expert faculty and peer group. I cleared the JEE Main and Advanced exams with good ranks.

Btech
